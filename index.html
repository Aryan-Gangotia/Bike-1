RIDING MY FIRST BIKE

When I was about to get a new bike for my birthday, I thought it was not right to get the one with the training wheels anymore.
I had been mocked enough about my bike so I thought it would be better that I had them removed.I gave my parents a list about how I wanted my bike to be.

  1. Without training wheels
  2. Nice tires
  3. Strong brakes
  4. Comfortable seat
  5. Black in colour
I just wanted it to be my dream bike and they got me exactly that. But when I laid my hands on the bike, I felt nervous, so much that I didn’t drive it for a week.
Then my father came into my room one day and told me something. He gave me some tips to ride the bike.
  • Always stay safe
  • Have confidence in yourself
  • Don’t be afraid to fall down
His words hit me pretty deep and I went to drive my bike. Now that I see myself driving my bike, it feels like the most liberating things in the world.
So for this, I couldn’t have thanked my father enough
